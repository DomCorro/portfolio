ABOUT

Hi, I'm Dominic

I'm Dominic, a photo and videographer based in Ottawa, with a deep-rooted passion for capturing the electrifying world of racing and everything automotive. My journey behind the lens is driven by a fascination with the interplay of light, speed, and emotion—elements that racing delivers in spades. Through my work, I aim to bring viewers into the heart of the action, whether it's the charged silence before the roar of engines at the start line, the balletic grace of a high-speed turn, or the intimate moments shared between drivers and their vehicles. I strive to tell the stories that unfold on the track and under the hood, highlighting not just the machinery but the human spirit that powers it. My dedication to my craft has connected me with a vibrant community of motorsport enthusiasts and has allowed me to share the unbridled joy and raw intensity of automotive culture through my eyes.
